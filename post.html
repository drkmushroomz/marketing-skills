The in-house vs agency debate has a clear answer for most DTC and ecommerce brands in 2026: a full-service performance agency delivers faster results at lower total cost than a fully staffed in-house team. That holds true for brands under $30M in annual revenue, where the math simply does not support hiring 5 specialists when a single agency relationship covers all of them. The debate gets more nuanced at scale, but the default position favors agencies for brands that need to move fast and prove ROAS quickly.
