# Train with Dave — Client Brief

## Brand Identity
- **Business:** Train with Dave (TWD) — boutique personal training studio in Orange County, CA
- **Owner:** David Cozzens
- **Website:** trainwithdaveoc.com
- **Brand Voice:** Authoritative yet approachable. Science-backed but not clinical. Aspirational but not intimidating. Think "expert sherpa" guiding you through fitness — not drill sergeant.
- **Tone:** Confident, warm, results-oriented. Lead with empathy for the client's journey, back it with credentials.

## Locations
- **Orange** — 2,000 sq ft boutique studio (lease renewing year-to-year)
- **Irvine** — second location
- **Laguna Hills** — service area
- Service area covers broader OC: Mission Viejo, Aliso Viejo, Tustin, Lake Forest, Newport Beach, Anaheim, Fullerton, Santa Ana, Huntington Beach, Yorba Linda

## Services & Offers
- 1-on-1 personal training (primary service)
- Custom workout programs
- Custom nutrition programs & education (nutritionist on staff)
- Accountability coaching
- Progress tracking via app
- **Current offer: Free consultation only** (no $49, $99, or $36 promos active)

## Revenue & Pricing
- Business does $30K-$40K/month
- Premium positioning — not a budget gym
- Sessions historically started at $54/session
- Membership option tested at $149/month (gym access + 1 session + program) — zero sign-ups

## Target Audience (Personas)
1. **35-50 Professional** — "I've been putting this off for too long." Situated, career-climber, middle class. Values expertise and accountability.
2. **35-50 Moms** — "I need to prioritize myself." Values community, feeling supported by peers.
3. **25-35 Millennial** — Higher income, mostly female but significant male segment. Parents may support financially. Asian and white demographics.
4. **Self-motivated Gen Z** — Competitive, self-driven.

## Unique Selling Points
- Trainers hold Kinesiology degrees / Exercise Physiologist credentials
- "More transformations than clients" — proven track record
- Voted Best Personal Training in OC (award-winning)
- Science-backed training methodology — not guesswork
- White-glove / VIP experience at a boutique studio
- Nutritionist on staff (not just trainers)
- No contracts
- Progress tracked via proprietary app

## Competitive Landscape
- Competes against: Equinox (premium but impersonal), Alloy Personal Training (franchise), 24 Hour Fitness (commodity), F45/OrangeTheory (group), independent trainers
- Key differentiator: scientific approach + personal attention + nutrition integration
- Strength over competitors: client results and transformations (measurable outcomes)
- Weakness: small facility (2,000 sq ft) vs. big-box gyms

## Content & SEO Context
- Strong organic presence — ranking well for "personal trainer [city]" terms
- High-performing blog content: "high protein fast food," "10 pounds of fat," macro-focused nutrition content
- YouTube channel approaching 10K subscribers — nutrition-focused content
- Organic traffic has largely replaced paid for lead gen at times

## Strategic Rules
- **NO price-based promos in ads** — only offer is free consultation
- Do NOT mention $49, $99, $36, or any dollar amount for sessions
- Free consultation is the only CTA offer
- **Train with Dave HAS contracts** — NEVER say "no contracts," "zero contracts," or similar
- Lead with outcomes and credentials, not price
- Location must be prominent — many searchers are geo-specific
- Avoid generic fitness language — emphasize the scientific/kinesiology angle
- "Award Winning Training 2025" is approved social proof
- "Voted Best in OC" is approved social proof
- Do not make medical claims
- Use "you/your" language, not "we/our" as subject
- Use Title Case in all headlines and descriptions
- Always include display paths (Path 1: keyword, Path 2: Free-Consult)
- Ensure primary keyword appears in 2-3 headlines per ad

## What's Working in Creative
- Emotional appeal drives highest CTR (2.3% vs. baseline 1%)
- Testimonial/transformation content resonates
- Nutrition angle differentiates from pure fitness competitors
- Text overlays on video ads essential (90% watch with no sound)

## What's NOT Working
- Price-focused promos generated clicks but zero conversions (hence removal)
- Generic location-broad terms waste spend (competitor searches)
- Informational keywords mixed with commercial intent = wasted budget
- BOFU-only messaging — needs TOFU brand-building too
