# Audience Targeting Reference

## Google Ads Audiences

### Search Campaign Targeting
- **Exact match** [keyword] -- most precise, lower volume
- **Phrase match** "keyword" -- moderate precision and volume
- **Broad match** keyword -- highest volume, use with smart bidding
- Audience layering: add in "observation" mode first, analyze, then switch to "targeting" for high performers
- RLSA: bid higher on past visitors, show different ads to returning searchers, exclude converters

### Display/YouTube Targeting
- Custom intent audiences (from converting keywords)
- In-market audiences (actively researching)
- Affinity audiences (interests/habits, better for awareness)
- Customer match (upload email lists)
- Similar/lookalike audiences

## Meta Audiences

### Core Audiences
- Interest targeting: layer with AND logic, start broad, exclude existing customers
- Demographics: age, gender, location (zip level), language
- Behaviors: purchase, device usage, travel, life events

### Custom Audiences
- Website visitors (180 days max, specific pages, time on site, frequency)
- Customer list (30-70% match rate, refresh regularly)
- Engagement (video viewers at 25/50/75/95%, page engagers, form openers)
- App activity

### Lookalike Audiences
- Use high-LTV customers as source (not all customers)
- 1% most similar, 1-3% good balance, 3-5% broader, 5-10% awareness only
- Exclude the source audience

## LinkedIn Audiences

### Job-Based: titles, functions, seniority, skills
### Company-Based: size, industry, company names (ABM), growth rate

### High-Performing Combinations
| Use Case | Targeting |
|----------|-----------|
| Enterprise sales | Company 1000+ + VP/CXO + Industry |
| SMB sales | Company 11-200 + Manager/Director + Function |
| Developer tools | Skills + Function + Company type |
| ABM | Company list + Decision-maker titles |

## Twitter/X Audiences
- Follower lookalikes, interest categories, keywords in tweets, conversation topics
- Lower CPMs than LinkedIn/Meta, less precise

## TikTok Audiences
- Demographics, interests, behaviors, device, custom/lookalike
- Younger skew (18-34), creative matters more than targeting

## Audience Size Guidelines
| Platform | Minimum | Ideal |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Google Search | 1,000+ | 5K-50K |
| Google Display | 100K+ | 500K-5M |
| Meta | 100K+ | 500K-10M |
| LinkedIn | 50K+ | 100K-500K |
| Twitter/X | 50K+ | 100K-1M |
| TikTok | 100K+ | 1M+ |

## Exclusion Strategy
Always exclude: existing customers (unless upsell), recent converters (7-14 days), bounced visitors (<10 sec), employees, irrelevant page visitors, competitors
